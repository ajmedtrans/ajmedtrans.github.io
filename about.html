About Us

What is Lighthouse?

Lighthouse is the leading non-emergency medical transportation provider servicing the panhandle and it's surrounding regions. We have firmly established a foundation that stands up to the very definition of high quality non-emergency medical transportation; getting you there safely, on time, and conveniently all at an unsurpassed level of affordability.

Lighthouse Values

We pride ourselves on being the shining beacon for those who are in need. We understand how difficult it is to deal with an injury or disability, realizing how mobility impairments can be a great inconvenience at home and even more difficult to manage when you're traveling. Here at Lighthouse, your family is our family, and above all else we prioritize our family's comfort, safety, and privacy.
